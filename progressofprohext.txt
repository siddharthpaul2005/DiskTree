Phase 2 — The Core Engine
What you're building:

thread_pool — manages worker threads for parallel directory scanning
progress — lock-free progress reporting from scanner to UI
scanner — the filesystem walker, the heart of the whole project
builder — rolls up sizes, sorts children, computes percentages
index — flat queryable structure over the tree
treemap — squarified layout algorithm
Why this order: thread_pool and progress are utilities the scanner depends on. Scanner produces the raw tree. Builder makes it useful. Index makes it queryable. Treemap is pure algorithm, no dependencies.
